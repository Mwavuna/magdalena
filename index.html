About the Group

This group supports individuals to understand and manage stress through mindfulness, journaling, interactive games, and discussion. We focus on building personal coping skills in a fun and safe space.

Session Plan

Session 1 — Mindfulness & Breathing

Warm-up games, breathing exercises, guided visualization to identify personal stress triggers.

Session 2 — Journaling & Reflection

Write about stress situations, discuss in pairs, share coping strategies in a safe circle.

Session 3 — Problem Solving & Scenarios

Role-play stressful scenarios, brainstorm coping options, team exercises for calm responses.

Session 4 — Creative Movement

Guided stretching, mindful walking, yoga-inspired exercises to relax and refocus.

Session 5 — Stress Games

Team games designed to reduce tension, build teamwork, and practice coping under fun pressure.

Session 6 — Reflection & Goals

Review techniques, share progress, set personal stress management goals for daily life.

Activities & Exercises

  • Mindful breathing and relaxation exercises
  • Journaling stress experiences and triggers
  • Role-playing everyday stress scenarios
  • Team-based stress reduction games
  • Yoga and gentle movement exercises
  • Reflection circles and goal-setting tasks
